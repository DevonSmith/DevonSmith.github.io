Inktober

This year I plan on participating in Inktober. I don't know where I might be putting my art but I'm looking into placing the images I create on some social network. Part of Inktober is to share what you create with the world. This year however, I'm not going to be drawing iwth traditional pens. This year I am going to use my tablet to draw all of the art for this year's inktober.

This year I'm going to try and follow all the prompts as much as possible and draw the first thing that comes to mind when I read the daily prompt. This should give me a pretty fluid set of images to create and should prevent me from stalling out from creative block.
